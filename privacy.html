Privacy Policy for Store Inventory

Effective Date: 19/01/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Store Inventory ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  1. a. User Account Information

    When you sign in with your registered Gmail ID, we collect basic account information [like : user name, email,photo] to provide you with access to our app.

  2. b. Google Drive Files

    Our app allows you to import files (.txt filtered) from your Google Drive. We only access and process files with the .txt extension.

3. How We Use Your Information

  1. a. User Authentication

    We use your registered Gmail ID for authentication purposes to provide you access to our app.

  2. b. File Processing

    We access and process .txt files from your Google Drive to allow you to create/overwrite new files and export them from the app to Google Drive.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Google Drive to facilitate file import and export. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. File processing is performed locally on your device, and only necessary information is transmitted securely between the app and Google Drive.

7. Policy Changes
